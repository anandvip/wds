Weed Detection System

Multispectral Camera System

Our weed detection system uses a multispectral camera mounted on a drone or tractor. This advanced sensor combines optical and spectral sensing technologies to accurately identify and measure weed coverage in your fields.

How it works:

  1. Image Capture: The multispectral camera takes high-resolution images of your fields.
  2. Spectral Analysis: It captures light in various wavelengths, including visible and near-infrared.
  3. Data Processing: Our software analyzes the spectral signatures to distinguish between crops and weeds.
  4. Weed Mapping: The system generates a weed coverage map for each field.
  5. Dashboard Integration: The processed data is fed into your crop monitoring dashboard.
